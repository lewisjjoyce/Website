Trimwop's Awakening

There once was a boy named trimwop who loved to paint pictures of his mum.
He had seven friends in total, if you include his clay models, zero if we exclude them.

Grammar was not Trimwop's greatest strength; he had never heard of a full stop.

"Mummy, Mummy, rotate your self 127o", exclaimed Trimwop in an exasperated manner.

VVVVVVVV. Trimwop's mum obliged, with the whirring of the rotation mechanism causing the ground to vibrate like a sock full of wasps.
"Request complete", was the monotonous drone that was supposed to resemble human articulation.

Trimwop's mother was no human. It was not even living. It was a vacuum cleaner drilled to a swegway with an Alexa voice control unit taped to the handle.
